138. {3. [mate tiSThati] etc.}

3. [sthA] or [vRt] with loc. = „keeping close to”, that is observing, obeying one’s precept, principle, judgment etc. Çâk. VI [na me zAsane tiSThasi] (you do not obey my order), Daç. 72 [mAturmate vartasva] (comply with the wish of your mother), Cp. Lat. stat promissis, stat sententiâ and Kâç, on P. 1, 3, 23 [mayi tiSThate] (it rests on me = I am to decide.)
