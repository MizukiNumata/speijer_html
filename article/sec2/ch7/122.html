Instances of this rule applied in literature if they occur at all, must be scanty. (*1)
A prâkrt passage in Mrcch. II, p. 68 daśaśuvaṇṇāha luddhu jūdakaru [= Skr. daśasuvarṇasya ruddo dyūtakaraḥ] (this player is detained for 10 suvarṇas) may afford an instance of it.
