The aorist with mā} is not restricted to prohibition. It does occasionally duty as an optative with negation. Mrcch. VIII, p. 280 vasantasene / anyasyām api jātau mā veśyā bhūs tvaṃ hi sundari / cāritryaguṇasaṃpanne jāyethā vimale kule}, R. 2, 30, 19 mā vaśaṃ dviṣatāṃ gamam}. It may even express a doubt (352): Kathâs. 42, 114 sahasā hi kṛtaṃ pāpaṃ kathaṃ mā bhūddipattaye} (how can a crime, recklessly perpetrated, fail to cause mishap?). Or anxiety: Pat. I, p. 418 maivaṃ vijñāyi} (lest one should decide thus).
